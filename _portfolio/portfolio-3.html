--- title: "Digital twining of granular materials to understand their mechanical behavior" excerpt: collection: portfolio --- In my research, I developed an end-to-end computational imaging and modelling workflow for constructing simulation-ready digital twins directly from image-derived microstructural data. This included a MATLAB-based framework that employs Voronoi-based remeshing to transform segmented microstructural images into numerically robust discretisations suitable for particle- and continuum-based simulations. I developed an integrated image-processing toolbox for the quantitative characterisation of both 2D and 3D microstructures, with applications to rock fracture networks, cementitious composites, and porous media, thereby providing a unified platform for geometry reconstruction, microstructural analysis, and numerical modelling.
